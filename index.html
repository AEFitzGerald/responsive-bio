Hi. I'm Anna.

I'm a Responsive Designer & Developer that loves the creative process. My work tools fit easily into a backpack; and I make my best work from whereever I roam. The digital age has made my life simpler, and I hope to share that freedom with others.

I was born in St. John, New Brunswick, where my father worked as a nuclear start up engineer. I was the only one in my family born in Canada, and it was a gift. I chose to go to college in my favorite Atlantic province, at Nova Scotia College of Art & Design.

My passions- I love the arts and I am enamored by many mediums- architecture, painting, food, music, and story telling... I am more passionate about movement arts and meditation than I am with sitting down and watching a show, but I will watch anything queer, and my favorite is Ru Paul's Drag Race.

Fact- I have spent the entirety of the COVID-19 pandemic in Boquete, Panama. This little town is peaceful and calm. And Volcano Baru towers in above my backyard. I am grateful.
